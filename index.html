Neurofeedback that is personalized, not generic

A noninvasive, computer-based training approach designed to support regulation, focus, and stress recovery.

Who it can support

Many people explore neurofeedback as a non-drug option for concerns tied to stress, attention, mood, and neurological patterns. It is often discussed for anxiety, trauma, ADHD, depression, migraines, and more.

How it starts

When appropriate, treatment planning may begin with a QEEG brain map, a pain-free procedure using sensors, so we can tailor protocols to your profile.
