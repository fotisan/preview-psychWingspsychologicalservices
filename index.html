Neurofeedback that is personalized, not generic

A noninvasive, computer based training approach designed to optimize brain wave regulation, focus, and stress recovery.

Who it can help

Many people want a non drug solution for concerns tied to brain wiring, including trauma, anxiety, ADHD, PTSD, depression, migraines, and autism spectrum related challenges.

How it starts

When appropriate, treatment planning can begin with a QEEG brain map, a pain free in office procedure using a cap and sensors, so we can see which areas may be overactive, underactive, or struggling with connectivity.

Custom treatment plans and individualized protocols
Multiple systems for different symptom profiles
Can be paired with therapy, supplements, or medication when needed

What to expect

Think of it as a workout for the brain. Over time, clients often report steadier focus, calmer reactions, and better stress recovery. Research is frequently cited as showing a strong success rate for many people, though results vary.

Do you offer telehealth in New Jersey?

Yes. Telehealth appointments are offered throughout the state of New Jersey, alongside in person sessions in Clifton.

How do I book a consultation?

Do you work with anxiety, panic, and OCD?

Yes. Anxiety related concerns are a major focus, including panic reactions and OCD support with evidence based approaches.

What is neurofeedback, in simple terms?

It is noninvasive brain training that aims to help the brain regulate itself more effectively, supporting focus, calm, and stress recovery.

How many neurofeedback sessions are typical?

It depends on goals and profile. Many clients begin noticing mild improvements within several sessions, with plans tailored over time.
